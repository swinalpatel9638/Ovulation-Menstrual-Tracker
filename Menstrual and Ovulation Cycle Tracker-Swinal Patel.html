PCOD (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)

⚡
PCOD affects the ovaries, leading to the release of immature eggs, causing hormonal imbalances. It can lead to various symptoms such as weight gain, acne, and excessive hair growth, which may require lifestyle changes and medical intervention for management.
⚡
Symptoms include weight gain, irregular periods, acne, and excessive hair growth. These symptoms can vary widely among individuals and may require a tailored approach for effective management.
⚡
Lifestyle changes like regular exercise, a balanced diet, and stress management can help control PCOD.
Learn more about PCOD

PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)

⚡
PCOS causes cyst formation in the ovaries due to excess androgen levels, affecting fertility and metabolism.
⚡
Common signs include irregular periods, acne, hair thinning, and difficulty in conceiving.
⚡
Long-term complications may include diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease, requiring a combination of medical treatment and lifestyle adjustments.
Learn more about PCOS

Menopause

⚡
Menopause is a natural phase marking the end of a woman’s reproductive years, typically occurring between 45-55 years.
⚡
Symptoms include mood swings, hot flashes, night sweats, and vaginal dryness. These symptoms can vary widely among women and may require lifestyle changes or medical advice for management.
⚡
A healthy diet, regular exercise, and possibly hormone replacement therapy (HRT) can help alleviate symptoms.
